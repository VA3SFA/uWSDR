This control sets the level of the microphone signal that is fed into the following modulators. Its effect on the output signal will also depend on the mode and the settings of the ALC and speech processor. The knob is moved by pressing the left mouse button and dragging the control around, the mark will follow the mouse around.

Each mode (including CW) has its own setting for the Mic Gain and this is remembered by the software.

This control is not operational when the SDR is receive only.
