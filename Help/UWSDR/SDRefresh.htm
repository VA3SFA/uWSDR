The Refresh Menu alters the speed that the Spectrum Display is updated. With a Panadapter type of display the refresh speed alters how smoothly the display updates, whereas on the Waterfall display is determines how much time it takes for the data to scroll off the top of the screen.

The refresh speeds available are:

  • 200 ms
  • 400 ms
  • 600 ms
  • 800 ms
  • 1 s

On slower computers the refresh speed can affect the efficiency of the overall SDR as processing and display the Spectrum Display is rather processor intensive.
