The Transmit Menu allows for the monitoring of the output signal levels at various stages along the transmit chain. The taps are at:

  • Microphone level, before any audio processing.
  • Compressed output, after the speech processor.
  • Post ALC level, after the ALC has limited the transmit level.
  • Power output, before the Power control.

The scale of the meter is arbitrary, but unlike the receive meter, the scale on transmit is linear.

This menu is not available if the SDR is receive only.
