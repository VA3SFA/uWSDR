The messages consist of standard letters; numbers and special characters, as well as special macros. The fixed message to be sent is selected with one of the buttons on the right hand side and the Transmit button pressed. The bottom message, labeled Real-Time is used for sending messages in real-time. The transmitter should be turned on manually, either from the Transmit Button on the GUI or via the serial port, and any valid character will be sent as they are entered.
