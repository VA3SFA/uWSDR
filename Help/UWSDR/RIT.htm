RIT (Receiver Incremental Tuning) allows for the receiver frequency to be changed slightly relative to the transmit frequency, this is limited to +/-5 kHz. It is switched on and off by the RIT button and the value is changed via the RIT knob. The frequency change is reflected in the main Frequency Display and the status in the Information Box. The knob is moved by pressing the left mouse button and dragging the control around, the mark will follow the mouse around.

If the SDR is receive only then the RIT button and RIT knob will be grayed out and will not work when pressed.
